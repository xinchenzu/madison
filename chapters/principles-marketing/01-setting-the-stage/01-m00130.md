Welcome to *Principles of Marketing*! Some people may mistakenly believe
that marketing skills can only be applied to marketing tasks, but the
fact is that marketing isn't only for marketers. Studying marketing is
essential in almost any career field, because it teaches you the basic
principles that connect people, brands, and businesses.

This textbook is divided into three units:

Unit 1: Setting the Stage

Unit 2: Understanding the Marketplace

Unit 3: Product, Promotion, Price, and Place

In this first section, we're going to set the stage for the remainder of
this textbook, by first exploring marketing as a discipline and
understanding the concept of customer value. Then we'll analyze the role
of strategic planning in marketing, because strategy defines how you
communicate that customer value to others.
