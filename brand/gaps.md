data, and AI to create measurable business impact."*

Every gap below is defined by comparing my **resume** to my **aspiration** — i.e.,
something my resume does NOT show. The "Madison build" column names a FUTURE thing
I would build to close the gap (a plan, not a past experience). Nothing here
claims work I have not done.
